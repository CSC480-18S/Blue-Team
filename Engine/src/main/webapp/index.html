Game Rules

The first tile must be placed on the center red square

Make words with your tiles to score points

Words can be played horizontally and vertically

When playing a new word it must be connected to a previously played word

Use the colored squared multipliers to get a higher score

Try and use words that are specific to Oswego for even more points

Buttons

Exit: Click on button to leave the game
Recall: Click on button to return tiles that have been placed on board back to your hand
Exchange: Click on button to select the tiles that you wish to replace
Shuffle: Click on button to randomly distribute your hand
Play: Click on button to play your tiles after they have been placed
